Larchmont Arts Festival: September 16

Lawn at Larchmont Public Library, 121 Larchmont Ave, Larchmont, NY. 10538
The Kenise Barnes/Julia B Fee Sotheby's Fine Art Award will be presented Saturday, September 16 in Constitution Park. The Festival is 12-5pm with over 50 artists in varied media. The works shown here are by artists in contention for the award. Come see the art and meet the artists! Also--music on on the stage in the park, art and music activities for kids. All art will be for purchase: paintings, photography, jewelry, ceramics, and more. Food, games, and Big Trucks will be in the street. Enjoy the day with your friends and neighbors!
